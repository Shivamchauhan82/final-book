About Bookshelf Ecommerce Solutions

Founded in May 2001, BookShelf provides all that is necessary to have an online bookstore or other store including domain hosting, email hosting, file transfer to upload inventory, web based administration, secure checkout, shopping cart, and more. All you need is your domain name, your inventory and how you want to accept payments.

BookShelf provides an easy and affordable ecommerce solution for your online bookstore or other retail store. We have provided the new, used, out-of-print, and antiquarian independent bookstore, as well as other retailers, with a full ecommerce store solutions since early 2001.
